Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-04

Time Budget is a personal time-tracking app. This policy explains what data the app handles, where it goes, and your rights.

What data we collect

  • Account identifier and email address — only if you sign in. Used to authenticate you and let you sync data across devices.
  • Your time-budget data — categories, budgets, timer activity, and daily history that you create inside the app. Stored under your account so it persists across sessions and devices.

If you choose not to sign in, your data lives only on your device in browser local storage and is not transmitted to any server.

What we do not collect

  • No location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access.
  • No tracking across other apps or websites. No advertising IDs.
  • No third-party analytics or marketing trackers.

Where your data is stored

Authenticated user data is stored in Supabase, a managed Postgres database service. Row-level security policies ensure each user can only read or write their own rows. Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS; data at rest is encrypted by Supabase's infrastructure.

Sign-in providers

The app offers Sign in with Apple, Sign in with Google, and email/password. When you sign in via a third-party provider, that provider sends us your email address and a stable identifier so we can recognize you. We do not receive your contacts, friends, or any other profile data.

Notifications

The app may schedule local notifications on your device to alert you when a category reaches its budget or a Pomodoro session ends. These run entirely on your device and are not sent through any external server.

Your rights

  • Delete your data — Settings → Clear all data wipes your categories and history.
  • Delete your account — Settings → Delete account permanently removes your account and all associated data from our database. This is immediate and irreversible.
  • Sign out — at any time. Local data on the device may remain unless you also Clear all data.

Children's privacy

Time Budget is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date above will change and we'll surface the change in the app where appropriate.
